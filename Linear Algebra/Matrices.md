# Matrices
---

A matrix is a special structure composed of numbers, it can be divided in $n$ rows and $m$ columns. It is a sort of "table" with custom properties, where every cell is a real value.

$$\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 &  3 &  4\\
5 & 6 &  7 &  8\\ 
8 & 9 & 10 & 11\\
\end{pmatrix}
\text{ is a 3x4 matrix} $$
